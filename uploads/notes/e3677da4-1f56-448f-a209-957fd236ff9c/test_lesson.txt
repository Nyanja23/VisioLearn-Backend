# Test Lesson: Introduction to Biology

## Unit 1: What are Living Things?
Living organisms are characterized by several key features:
- They consume energy
- They reproduce
- They respond to stimuli
- They adapt to their environment
- They maintain homeostasis

## Unit 2: Classification of Living Things
The major kingdoms of life include:
1. Animalia - animals like humans, insects, birds
2. Plantae - plants like trees, flowers, grass
3. Fungi - mushrooms, yeast, mold
4. Protista - single-celled organisms
5. Archaea - ancient microorganisms
6. Bacteria - common single-celled organisms

## Unit 3: Cell Structure
All living organisms are made of cells, which are the basic units of life.
- Prokaryotic cells: simpler, no nucleus (bacteria)
- Eukaryotic cells: complex, with nucleus (animals, plants)
